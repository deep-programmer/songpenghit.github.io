Biography

Peng Song is a postdoctoral researcher at EPFL, working with Mark Pauly since October 2017. From 2014 to 2017, he worked as an associate researcher in University of Science and Technology of China. From 2013 to 2014, he worked as a research fellow in Nanyang Technological University, Singapore.

Peng Song received his B.S. from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2007, M.S. from Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen) in 2010, and Ph.D. from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in 2013.
